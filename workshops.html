Workshops

Over the last couple of years, we've kept ourselves busy building courses, facilitating supervision, and working with undergraduate psychology students. Now, we're adding something new to the mix!

The Edge is expanding into workshops for early- to mid-career therapists, bringing together theory, practice, and meaningful conversations that make us better therapists. We're quite excited for this one and can't wait to learn alongside you.

Workshop 1: What Remains: Working with Grief

Module 1 · What Loss Leaves Behind: Understanding Grief

Grief is much more than bereavement, and it rarely follows a neat path. In this module, we'll explore the many forms grief can take, how culture and context shape our experience of loss, and the assumptions we often carry about grieving. Through reflective exercises and discussion, we'll broaden our understanding of grief and the many ways it appears in our clients' lives.

Module 2 · What the Body Knows: Neurobiology and Models of Grief

We know grief isn't just emotional and that it lives in the brain, body, and our relationships. In this module, we'll explore what happens neurobiologically when we grieve, alongside key grief models brought to life through therapy room examples. Rather than treating these models as fixed stages, we'll focus on using them as flexible frameworks to better understand our clients' experiences.

Module 3 · When Clients Get Stuck: Working with Common Themes in Grief

Instead of learning therapeutic modalities in isolation, we'll begin with what clients actually bring into the room. We'll explore themes such as feeling "stuck," loss of meaning and identity, guilt, self-blame, avoidance, and emotional numbness. Drawing from Narrative Therapy, CBT, ACT, Compassion-Focused Therapy, and Gestalt approaches, we'll discuss practical ways of responding to these challenges in therapy.

Module 4 · What the Work Asks of Us: Therapeutic Presence in Grief

Working with grief isn't only about knowing what to do, it also asks something of us as therapists. In this final module, we'll reflect on the therapeutic stance that supports grief work, explore common therapist pitfalls, and work through case vignettes to consider how we remain present, relational, and compassionate when there is nothing to fix.

FAQs❗❓

Will you offer group discounts?

No, but don't let that stop you. Just bring yourself. We'll take care of the rest.

Will I get a job with Kaha Mind after this workshop?

While we can't guarantee a job, we're always on the lookout for the best minds. If you think you fit the bill, write to us at careers@kahamind.com.

I'm still studying; can I apply?

Unfortunately, if you are still pursuing your Undergrad or Masters, no. If you are just starting out, or have a few years of experience, we can't wait to meet you.

Is this course fully online, in-person, or hybrid?

This cohort will be conducted online. If you're joining us online, all you need is a laptop and a good internet connection. Keeping your video on during sessions is mandatory.

Can I record the sessions?

Nice try, but no <3 We promise it's worth showing up live.
